TAW-Foley: Triple Attention Waveform Diffusion Model for Foley Sound Generation

Runwu Shi, Chang Li, Jiahui Li, Jiang Wang, Ragib Amin Nihal, Kazuhiro Nakadai

GitHub Repository
Abstract: Recent advancements in deep generative models have greatly improved Foley sound generation, especially with diffusion-based methods that directly generate high-quality waveforms. However, the backbones of these models, typically based on convolutional U-Nets or temporal self-attention on spectrograms, have limited ability to capture the fine-grained temporal and frequency structures of Foley sounds, thereby constraining their expressive capacity. To address this, we introduce a frequency-domain diffusion model for raw waveform generation. Our model employs three attention mechanisms to capture features at different scales: intra-frame, intra-subband, and full-temporal. This approach not only enhances generation quality but also drastically reduces model parameters, leading to more efficient audio modeling. Furthermore, our model design supports more detailed temporal-event control, allowing for precise control over the generated sounds. Both objective and subjective experiments validate the feasibility of our proposed model.
